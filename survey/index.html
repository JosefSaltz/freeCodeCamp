How Much Do Online Surveys Annoy You?

To help gauge the best way to annoy our userbase, we would appreciate it if you could take the time to complete a quick survey.

  1. Q: When prompted how often do you feel like completing online surveys?
  2. Q: When requested how frequently are you willing to fill out online surveys?
  3. Q: Do you often feel like surveys are repeating the same question?
  4. Q: How annoyed do you feel when it feels like you've answered the same question several times?
  5. Q: Do you often feel like the questions are created just to fill the space?
  6. Q: How annoyed fo you feel when you complete many questions and click submit to be prompted with more questions on the next page?
  7. Q: On average how much of a waste of time do you feel filling out a form takes?
  8. Q: How sincere would you rate your answers?
  9. Q: How much more satisfied would you be if the results of this survey determined the author's continued employment?
  10. Q: Do you often feel like it's unnecessary to choose from a spectrum of choices for yes or no questions?
  11. Q: Would knowing that the survey contains an uneven number of questions bother you?
